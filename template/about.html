About Stride Club

Welcome
To Stride Club

Stride club is the sports club of Abiruchi in Invertis University, whose objective is inculcating the spirit of sportsmanship and propagating the spirit of team work, harmony and peace with the help of sports. Stride club organize events like IPL, Aaveg, Aswarun in which participants take part in various games like Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Table Tennis, Athletics, Basketball, Badminton and Kabaddi and all indoor games.

Aim of our Website

Our aim is to connect students from different campuses all over India by making them aware of Sports and can participate in sports events online that are organized by Invertis University.
