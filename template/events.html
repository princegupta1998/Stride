AAVEG (Hustle and Heart set us apart)

Aaveg is a national level sports meet organized by Invertis University. We aim at inculcating the spirit of sportsmanship and propagating the spirit of team work, harmony and peace. Over a span of 3 high-intensity days, the festival will hold a large number of game events like Cricket (M), Football (M), Volleyball (M&W),Table Tennis (M&W), Athletics (M&W), Basketball (M&W), Badminton (M&W) and Kabaddi (M). The participants of every sport have a great opportunity to showcase their talents and grab the exciting prizes.

IPL(Play like a Champion Today)

The Invertis Premier League (IPL) is a popular sport event of Invertis University Barielly contested during April and May of every year organized by Stride Club.The league was founded by "------"in "-----". ASWARAN (Rise as ONE)

Aswaran is a group of sporting events of Invertis University that involves competitive running, jumping and threwing contested during August and September of every year organized by Stride Club. Aswaran include all types of athletics competitions like road running, long Jump, high jump, javelin throw and race walking.The participants of every sport have a great opportunity to showcase their talents and grab the exciting prizes.
